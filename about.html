About Connect

A small social app, made on purpose.

Why this exists

Most social apps want your attention for as long as they can keep it. Connect is being built the other way around. The goal isn't time spent. It's whether you feel better, sharper, or more in touch after you close the app than you did when you opened it.

How it's built

Connect is a Flutter app with a Supabase backend, shipped on iOS and Android. Features land one at a time, and stay only if they belong. The features page lists what's in today.

Who's making it

Built by Mahdi. Solo project, no team, no roadmap deck. If you want to reach out, find me through the contact link in the app or on the GitHub repo.
