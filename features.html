What's inside Connect

A short tour of the parts that are working today. New things land when they're ready, not because there's a calendar.

Stories that fade

Post a moment and it lives for 24 hours. Replies thread back to the story, and if someone reposts you, the original sticks around long enough for the conversation to make sense. After that, it's gone.

Debates that matter

Quick takes on real questions, tagged by topic and surfaced to people who care about them. The feed mixes in debates that match your interests, not just whatever was loudest today.

Quizzes built from real content

Short adaptive quizzes from channel shorts and shared links, three to five questions long. No padding, no trick questions, just a quick check on what stuck.

Tide, a place to breathe

Hold the screen, follow the rhythm, watch the partner shimmer in time. No accounts, no streaks, no leaderboard. Just a minute or two to come back to yourself.

A feed that respects your time

Refresh reorders what you've already seen instead of hiding it. The Refine card moves around so you actually notice it. Nothing pretends to be urgent.

Pro, when you want it

Ads support the free tier. Pro turns them off and unlocks the parts that need a bit more room. Same app either way.
