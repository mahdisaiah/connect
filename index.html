01 Mobile product iOS — May 2026

A social platform built
for real conversation.

Connect is a modern social platform where people share posts, moments and debates around real content from across the web. It puts interaction ahead of consumption. React, discuss and challenge ideas instead of just scrolling.

Clean, immersive design throughout. Connect turns social media back into a place for expression, conversation and engagement that means something.

Platform iOS at launch · Android soon

04 Offline On-device · no servers

When the network drops,
a small room opens.

Four games. No accounts, no servers, no scores beyond what's on the screen. They run when the rest of the app can't.

  • 01
    Net.

    Rotate to mend the network. An endless brain puzzle on a procedural grid, rooted in the Connect mark. Wider, twistier with every level.

    1 playerProceduralEndlessOn-device
  • 02
    Tide.

    Breathe with a stranger. The resonance pacer, lifted out of the app into its own room. Five seconds in, five seconds out.

    1 playerResonance6 BPMHaptic
  • 03
    Spin.

    A wheel decides who must answer. Truth or dare, with a casino-style ratcheting wheel that decelerates over seven seconds.

    2 to 10Same phoneTruth / DareHaptic
  • 04
    Faker.

    One of you knows nothing. The phone passes around. Two lines each, then a vote, then the faker's one guess.

    4 to 10Same phoneDrawingSocial deduction

06 The maker

A small social app, made by one person, on purpose.

Built by Mahdi, in Paris. No team, no pitch deck, no public roadmap. One careful thing at a time. Connect is a Flutter app on iOS and Android, with Supabase as the backend and a Groq agent powering the debate tag system.
