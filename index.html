What's in Connect

A small set of things, done with care.

Stories

Share a moment for 24 hours. Replies tie back to the story so context never goes missing.

Debates

Quick takes on real questions, with a feed that surfaces topics you actually care about.

Quizzes

Short, adaptive quizzes built from posts and shorts. Three to five questions, no filler.

Tide

A breathing screen with a partner you can feel. Hold the screen, follow along, come back to yourself.

Built one feature at a time.

Connect is an ongoing project, not a launched product. Things ship when they're ready, and only if they belong. The full list lives on the features page.
