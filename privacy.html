Privacy

A plain-language summary of what Connect collects and why.

Draft, replace before publishing

What we collect

  • Account info you give us: email, username, profile photo.
  • Content you create: posts, stories, debates, quiz answers, messages.
  • Basic usage data: device type, app version, crash reports.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't share content with advertisers.
  • We don't track you across other apps or sites.

Where it lives

Your data sits in Supabase, hosted in the EU. Auth, storage, and database access are gated by row-level security.

Ads

Free accounts see ads through Google AdMob and through our own ad system. AdMob may use device identifiers as described in their policy. Pro accounts don't see ads at all.

Your rights

You can delete your account from inside the app. Deleting removes your profile, posts, stories, and messages. Some logs are retained for up to 30 days for safety and abuse review.

Contact

Questions about privacy go to hello@example.com.
