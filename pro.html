02.

Common questions.

What people ask before subscribing.

Will any feature ever move behind Pro? +

No. Existing features stay free. Pro adds room and removes ads. New features land in Free first, with Pro getting early access during the beta window.

What kind of ads does Free see? +

Three formats: native cards in the feed, occasional full-screen interstitials, and an app-open ad on cold start. There is also a small in-house ad system for community causes. None track you across other apps.

Can I cancel any time? +

Yes. Through the App Store or Play Store, the same place any other subscription lives. Your Pro status holds until the end of the current billing period.

Is there a student or yearly plan? +

A yearly tier at a small discount is on the way. Student pricing is being looked at for the next release window.

Does Pro change Tide or the offline games? +

Not a thing. Tide and the offline four are identical in both tiers. They live on-device. Pro doesn't add or remove anything there.
