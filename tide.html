01.

Why this protocol.

Resonant breathing at six breaths per minute lifts vagal tone, maximises baroreflex gain, and shifts autonomic balance toward the parasympathetic state. It works because the cardiovascular and respiratory systems share a natural resonance there.

Citations

Built on real research.

The protocol is grounded in three lines of work. Lehrer and Gevirtz (2014) laid out the resonance-frequency model. Steffen, Austin, DeBarros and Brown (2017) confirmed acute effects. Elliott (2008) connected the practice to long-term autonomic shift.

No hold phase, equal in and out. Maximally entraining, accessible to first-timers.

In one minute

What changes.

Heart-rate variability rises. Sympathetic drive falls. The interoceptive signal sharpens. None of this is felt as a "high," only as a soft un-clenching, often surprising in how quick it is.

No gamification. No badges. Just the protocol, faithfully.
